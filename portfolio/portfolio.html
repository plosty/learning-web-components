About

Details about my background, history and future plans.

Work History

My technical background is mainly in Quality Assurance.

After initially joining Cap Gemini as a graduate, I joined the support team of a small startup company called Application Networks, who provided financial software to some of the worlds largest investment banks.

Over the next few years, as the company was bought and merged with Reuters and eventually Misys, I moved more in specialising in Quality Assurance

In 2015, I moved away from financial software to become QA manager to form a small team at Piriform, the makers of the PC optimisation software CCleaner.

As a team, we focused on making sure our much-loved products performed to the standards demanded by over 2m users. Automated tests helped to achieve this

In 2017 I joined V1, a new technology consultancy based in San Francisco to focus on building a BDD approach on our development process with our partners.

Education

My first degree was in Management Science at Warwick Universty.

Following that, whilst working, I completed my MSc in Computer Science at Birkbeck University, who specialise in part-time study.

Finally in 2016 I completed my BSc in Mathemantics and Statistics at the Open University, as a part-time distance learning course.

Future Aims and Ambitions

I have a keen interest in developing my technical skills and knowledge, which was why I signed up for the BOV Academy of Innovation and Programming Full Stack Developer course

My aim by the end of 2018 is to become a proficient Full Stack engineer, specialising in JavaScript, React and Node

This portfolio site showcases some of the work I have produced during the completion of the course.

If you have any questions, please feel free to get in touch via any of the methods in the Contact section
